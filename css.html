Beauty of Nature

Just living is not enough... Adopt the peaceace of nature: her secret is patience. Wherever you go, no matter what the weather, always bring your own sunshine.

Some Quotations on Beauty of Nature

  • Look deep into nature, and then you will understand everything better        - Albert Einstein 
  • One touch of nature makes the whole world kin       - William Shakespeare
  • Those who find beauty in all of nature will find themselves at one with the secrets of life itself          -L. Wolfe Gilbert

In every walk in with nature one receives far more than he seeks. The day, water, sun, moon, night - I do not have to purchase these things with money. Looking at beauty in the world, is the first step of purifying the mind.

Nature always wears the colors of spirit
